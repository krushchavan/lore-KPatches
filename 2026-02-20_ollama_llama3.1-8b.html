Ongoing Submitted 2026-02-19 [PATCH 1/1] btrfs: set BTRFS_ROOT_ORPHAN_CLEANUP during subvol create Needs Work LLM Under Review

This patch sets the BTRFS_ROOT_ORPHAN_CLEANUP flag during subvolume creation to prevent orphan cleanup issues that can occur when a subvolume is deleted but its dentry cache remains. The problem arises from concurrent deletion of inode and orphan items, which can lead to ENOENT errors and fs aborts. The patch addresses this issue by ensuring that the BTRFS_ROOT_ORPHAN_CLEANUP flag is set during subvolume creation, allowing for safe deletion of subvolumes even when their dentry cache is dropped.
