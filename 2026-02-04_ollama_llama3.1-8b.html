Ongoing Submitted 2026-01-29 [PATCH 0/9] cxl: explicit DAX driver selection and hotplug 9 patches Needs Work LLM New Version Expected

This patch series addresses two issues in CXL (Compute Express Link) region management: the lack of control over hotplug online policy and the inability to choose between device_dax and dax_kmem modes at region creation time. It introduces new infrastructure for explicit DAX driver selection and hotplug policy configuration, allowing users to specify the desired policy when creating CXL regions. The series includes new drivers that provide a two-stage binding process with user-configurable policy, enabling explicit control over both the DAX driver mode and memory hotplug policy.
