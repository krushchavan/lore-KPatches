Patch summary
This patch series hardcodes the pool mode to per-node, removing other modes and addressing issues such as scheduling RPCs to pools with no threads. It also implements suggestions from Neil to eliminate the serv.sv_nrpools field, which has the side effect of reducing the modulus operations in the svc_pool_for_cpu() hotpath. The patches include fixes for comment cleanups, shortcoming of existing code, and auto-thread-placement case. The approach taken is to ensure that each populated node has at least one thread and prevent routing requests to empty pools.
