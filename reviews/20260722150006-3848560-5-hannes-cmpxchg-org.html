Patch summary
This patch fixes a 'reclaim storm' issue in the Linux kernel's page allocation system, specifically in defrag_mode. The problem occurs when non-movable requests spin in reclaim, causing excessive swapping and pressure spikes on certain workloads. To fix this, the patch sends non-movable requests into pageblock reclaim/compaction, allowing them to help move pages along and meet the ALLOC_NOFRAGMENT bar. This approach is a temporary solution until a more comprehensive fix can be implemented to address scaling and concurrency limitations in compaction.
