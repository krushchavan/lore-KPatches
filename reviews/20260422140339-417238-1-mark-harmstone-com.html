[PATCH] btrfs: don't force DIO writes to be serialized

View on lore.kernel.org →
Submitted: 2026-04-22  ·  Last comment: 2026-04-23
Active on: 2026-04-23 • 2026-04-22
Patch summary
A patch to prevent forced serialization of DIO writes in Btrfs, which was causing performance issues.
Mark Harmstone 2026-04-23 Positive LLM
Identified a performance issue in Btrfs where DIO writes are being serialized, leading to significant drops in write performance. Proposed the patch to address this issue.
