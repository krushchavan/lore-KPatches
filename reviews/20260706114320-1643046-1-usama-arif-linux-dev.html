[PATCH v2 0/6] mm: preparatory patches for PMD level swap entries

View on lore.kernel.org →
Submitted: 2026-07-06
Active on: 2026-07-06
Patch summary
This patch series is a preparatory step for introducing PMD level swap entries in the Linux kernel. It cleans up existing PMD softleaf helpers and call sites, adding new helper functions and refactoring open-coded logic to make it easier to review and merge the follow-up series that introduces PMD swap entries. The patches are safe to merge independently and do not introduce any new page-table entry types or install new page-table entry types. They focus on defensive checks, mechanical renames, and helper additions to preserve current migration/device-private behavior.
