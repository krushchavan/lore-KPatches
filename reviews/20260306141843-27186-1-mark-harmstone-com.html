Patch summary
This patch fixes a use-after-free bug in the btrfs move_existing_remap() function by introducing a new variable to hold the block group reference throughout the entire function, eliminating the need for repeated lookups and put operations.

> There is a potential use-after-free in move_existing_remap(): we're calling

> near the end. This also means we're not doing a lookup twice for the
