Patch summary
This patch series addresses six pre-existing bugs in the userfaultfd and pagemap code, which were previously carried as part of a larger feature series. The issues include missing updates to hardware Dirty/Accessed bits, incorrect handling of gigantic hugetlb pages, self-deadlocks when scanning unpopulated hugetlb ranges, loss of uffd-wp markers during PMD permission downgrades, and out-of-bounds writes in the vma_flags_t structure. Each bug is fixed independently and applies to the mm-new code directly, with some patches also carrying Cc: stable@ for backporting. The approach taken involves correcting specific functions and data structures to ensure accurate and safe handling of userfaultfd and pagemap operations.
