Patch summary
This patch series moves the concept of 'stock' from mem_cgroup to page_counter, which is an optimization that reduces memory allocation on systems with fewer leaf cgroups. The worst-case scenario involves a workload charging to all CPUs once, resulting in overcharging and potential memory pressure issues. However, this scenario is rare and can be mitigated by the existing recovery mechanism in try_charge_memcg. The patch also highlights a gap in user interpretability when workloads litter unused charge everywhere without triggering a drain_all_stock event.
