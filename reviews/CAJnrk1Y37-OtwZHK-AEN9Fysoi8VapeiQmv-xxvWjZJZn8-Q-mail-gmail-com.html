[PATCH 0/2] fuse: Fix possible memleak at startup with immediate teardown

View on lore.kernel.org →
Submitted: 2026-04-10
Active on: 2026-04-10
Patch summary
This patch addresses a potential memory leak and hang issue in the FUSE (Filesystem in Userspace) kernel module. The problem arises from concurrent access to the queue references, leading to ent entries being leaked or causing the system to wait indefinitely for queue references to drop to zero. The patch ensures that the async teardown worker is running, guaranteeing that the ent entry will be properly torn down and preventing memory leaks and hangs.
