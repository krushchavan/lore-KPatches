Patch summary
This patch series addresses a performance issue in the Linux kernel's memory management system, specifically when vm.defrag_mode is enabled. The problem arises from non-movable requests spinning and reclaiming unproductively due to kswapd/kcompactd being overwhelmed. To fix this, the patches modify the allocator slowpath to allow non-movable requests to participate in pageblock production, which has been successfully tested on an affected workload for two weeks.
