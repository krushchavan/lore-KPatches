[PATCH] btrfs: fix use-after-free in move_existing_remap()

View on lore.kernel.org →
Active on: 2026-02-25
Patch summary
This patch fixes a potential use-after-free bug in the btrfs move_existing_remap() function by moving the call to btrfs_put_block_group() to the end of the function, eliminating the need for an additional lookup of the block group further down.
