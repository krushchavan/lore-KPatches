[PATCH RFC 0/3] locking: contended_release tracepoint instrumentation

View on lore.kernel.org →
Submitted: 2026-03-04  ·  Last comment: 2026-03-05
Active on: 2026-03-05 • 2026-03-04
Patch summary
The existing contention_begin/contention_end tracepoints fire on the waiter side. The lock holder's identity and stack can be captured at contention_begin time (e.g. perf lock contention --lock-owner), but this reflects the holder's state when a waiter arrives, not when the lock is actually released. This series adds a contended_release tracepoint that fires on the holder side when a lock with waiters is released. This provides: - Hold time estimation: when the holder's own acquisition was contended, its contention_end (acquisition) and contended_release can be correlated to measure how long the lock was held under contention.
