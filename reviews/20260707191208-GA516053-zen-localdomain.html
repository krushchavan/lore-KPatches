Patch summary
This patch addresses a regression in Btrfs where PAGECACHE_TAG_DIRTY is never cleared, leading to potential issues such as csum corruption when mmap writes are allowed on folios under writeback. The problem arises from the removal of folio_clear_dirty_for_io() and folio_mkclean(), which previously ensured that folios were write-protected during writeback. To fix this, the patch likely takes a lock in writeback to prevent races between truncate and writeback operations, ensuring that csums are correctly computed.
