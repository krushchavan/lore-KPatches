Patch summary
Discussion around adding a sysfs interface for atomic hotplug in dax/kmem, specifically regarding the handling of MMOP_UNPLUGGED state.
David Hildenbrand (Arm) 2026-06-09 Positive LLM
Proposed a simpler solution by special-casing the KMEM-special value in dax_kmem_parse_state().
Signals: POSITIVE
Gregory Price 2026-06-09 Needs Work LLM
Raised concerns about adding MMOP_UNPLUGGED as a valid state, suggesting it could lead to permanent unplug without recovery. Proposed special-casing the value instead.
