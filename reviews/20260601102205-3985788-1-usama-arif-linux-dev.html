Patch summary
This patch improves large folio readahead performance for executable memory by simplifying the logic and capping the maximum THP (Transparent Huge Pages) order to 2M. The changes are based on benchmark results that show a significant improvement in cold fault and random access times, with an average reduction of 50% and 23%, respectively. The patch builds upon previous revisions and includes suggestions from multiple contributors to ensure correctness and performance.
