Patch summary
This patch series addresses issues caused by device-private PMDs in walk callbacks. Device-private PMDs can be created when an HMM-based GPU driver migrates anonymous THPs to device memory, leading to unexpected behavior and potential crashes. The patches modify various MM walk callbacks to skip or handle these device-private PMDs correctly, preventing VM_BUG_ONs, oops, and incorrect LRU isolation.
