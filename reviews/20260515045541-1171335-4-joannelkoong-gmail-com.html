Patch summary
This patch fixes a bug in the FUSE (Filesystem in Userspace) implementation of io_uring, where cancelled entries were being moved to the ent_in_userspace list and causing a crash when dereferenced. The fix involves freeing the entry and dropping queue_refs directly in fuse_uring_cancel(), which is safe because cancel is the cancel handler itself. Additionally, the patch removes a gate that prevented fuse_uring_abort() from flushing requests and stopping queues if there were still queued requests, as cancelled entries may have already dropped queue_refs.
Joanne Koong (author) 2026-05-15 Neutral LLM
The author is addressing a concern that the patch may introduce a deadlock due to the io_uring submitter's task context holding the uring lock, which could lead to issues when calling io_uring_cmd_done(). The author agrees that keeping things simple by just aborting might not be the best approach and thinks it might lead to more trouble.
