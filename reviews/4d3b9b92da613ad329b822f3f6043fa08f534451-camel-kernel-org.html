[PATCH v2 001/110] vfs: introduce kino_t typedef and PRIino format macro

View on lore.kernel.org →
Active on: 2026-03-04 • 2026-03-03
Patch summary
This patch widens the inode fields in the audit system to use u64 type, allowing for larger inode numbers and improving the system's ability to handle large file systems.
Jeff Layton (author) 2026-03-03 Neutral LLM
Author acknowledged that the removal of kino_t was not clearly explained in the cover letter and assured it will be removed at the end of the series, either by being factored out or going away entirely if HCH's one-big-patch plan succeeds.
Show original comment 
I think I didn't make this clear enough in the cover letter, but kino_t
is removed at the end of the series. It's just there to support the
change during the interim.

If HCH gets his way to do the changes as one big patch, it'll go away
entirely.
-- 
Jeff Layton <jlayton@kernel.org>
