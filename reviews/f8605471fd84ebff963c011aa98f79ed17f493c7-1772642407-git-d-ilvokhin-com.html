Patch summary
This patch series adds a new tracepoint called contended_release to track when a lock is released due to contention. The tracepoint is added to the slowpath unlock paths of various sleepable locks, including mutexes, rtmutexes, semaphores, and rwsems.
Dmitry Ilvokhin 2026-03-04 Neutral LLM
Added the tracepoint to various slowpath unlock paths, including mutexes and rtmutexes. The tracepoint fires when there are blocked waiters being woken.
