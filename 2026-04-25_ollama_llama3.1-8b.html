[PATCH v2] mm/lruvec: preemptively free dead folios during lru_add drain Neutral llm-chunked-no-replies Awaiting Review

This patch eliminates unnecessary lock acquisitions by preemptively freeing dead folios during the lru_add process. It does this by using folio_ref_freeze() to filter out folios that are about to be immediately removed, and then moving them onto a temporary batch for deferred cleaning. This approach reduces contention on the lruvec lock and improves system-wide memory stats, such as direct reclaim scanning and allocation stalls, with no observed regressions in request service or latency.
